Local SEO Checklist 2026

Actionable steps to rank your business higher on Google Maps and Search.

1. Google Business Profile

Claim and verify your Google Business Profile (GBP).
Ensure NAP (Name, Address, Phone) matches your website exactly.
Select the most relevant primary business category.
Upload high-quality photos (Interior, Exterior, Team, Work).

2. On-Page SEO

Include your target city + service keyword in the Title Tag.
Use one H1 tag containing your main keyword.
Add "City, Province" to your meta description.
Embed a Google Map on your contact page.

3. Technical SEO

Ensure website is Mobile-Friendly (Google Mobile Test).
Secure site with HTTPS (SSL Certificate).
Improve Page Load Speed (compress images).

4. Authority & Content

Create location-specific service pages (e.g., "Plumbing Toronto").
Get listed in local directories (YellowPages, Yelp, etc.).
Ask satisfied customers for Google Reviews.
