Why Redesign is Often the Wrong First Move

Avoid expensive mistakes by understanding your baseline.

Redesigns are appealing because they promise a visible result. But a redesign without data is just a cosmetic update. If you don't understand your current performance, you risk:

  • Removing pages that quietly drive most of your traffic.

  • Breaking user journeys that your sales team relies on.

  • Repeating old mistakes in a newer, prettier wrapper.

A digital audit lets you separate cosmetic issues from structural ones. It gives you the evidence to decide whether you need a full rebuild or focused, high-impact improvements.
